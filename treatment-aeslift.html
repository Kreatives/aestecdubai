The treatment

What is the Aēslift™?

The Aēslift™ is a complete, scientifically grounded way of refreshing the face with hyaluronic acid, placed in small, precise amounts to lift and restore rather than simply to fill. Instead of treating a single line or one area, the specialist reads the whole face, its contours, symmetry and proportion, and rebuilds what time has quietly taken away.

Every placement is made under ultrasound, in the exact layer where it belongs, which is what keeps the result looking like you on a rested day rather than like a treatment. It is minimally invasive, with no scarring and no real downtime, and the effect is reversible should you ever wish to adjust it.

Results typically last between eighteen and twenty-four months, after which a small touch-up maintains them. The fillers also encourage the skin's own collagen, so the face continues to age gently and naturally from there.

Frequently asked

Questions about the Aēslift™.

Why isn't the Aēslift always available?

The Aēslift is performed by a specialist based in Amsterdam who travels to Dubai for it. Rather than dilute the treatment, we keep it on a waitlist and run it only when the specialist is here.

How long will I wait?

It depends on the schedule of the next visit. When you register your interest, we will tell you when the specialist is next expected and place you on the list accordingly.

What does the Aēslift actually treat?

It is a complete, tailored facial treatment rather than a single product in a single area. The specialist balances the whole face under ultrasound, which is why it begins with assessment rather than a fixed plan.

Why is there no fixed price?

Because no two Aēslifts are the same. The treatment is planned around your individual face, so the investment is confirmed only after the consultation, with no surprises on the day.

What happens after I register?

We contact you to arrange a consultation, confirm whether the Aēslift suits you, and let you know the dates of the next specialist visit. There is no obligation at any point.

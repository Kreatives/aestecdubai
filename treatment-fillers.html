Fillers may be right for you if…

Your face has lost contour and looks flatter than it once did.

You look tired even when you are well rested.

Folds around the nose and mouth have deepened.

How it works

Seeing what was, for years, invisible.

Visualisation

The ultrasound screen shows the vasculature and the tissue layers in real time, so the doctor sees what would otherwise be invisible to a needle approaching from above.

Precision

Each filler is placed in the exact layer where it belongs, neither superficially near a vessel nor too deep against the bone. That is the difference between a result that looks composed and one that looks corrected.

Recovery

Patients leave with fewer bruises, less swelling, and a result that settles more naturally over the following weeks, because the product went where it was meant to go.

The process

Three appointments, never rushed.

01

Consult

The doctor reads your face and listens before suggesting anything. The doctor may tell you a treatment is not right for you, and there is no charge for that conversation.

02

Treatment

On a separate day, the filler is placed under ultrasound guidance using original, certified European product. Most appointments take under an hour.

03

Aftercare

We see you again after two weeks to review how the result has settled, and to make any small adjustment at no additional cost.

Frequently asked

Questions patients ask about fillers.

Does it hurt?

Most patients describe only mild discomfort. The products contain a local anaesthetic, and the ultrasound guidance means fewer passes of the needle, which keeps the experience comfortable.

How long do the results last?

Hyaluronic acid fillers placed with our protocols typically last between eighteen and twenty-four months, after which a small touch-up with a fraction of the original quantity maintains the result.

Will it look natural?

That is the entire point of how we work. A successful treatment is one your friends never identify as a treatment at all, and if a request would not look natural on your face, the doctor will say so.

Can the filler be removed?

Hyaluronic acid fillers are reversible. If you are ever unhappy with a result, it can be dissolved with an enzyme, which is one of the reasons we work only with these formulations.

Is it safe during pregnancy?

We do not perform filler treatments during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. The doctor will always ask, and will postpone treatment until it is appropriate.

Why do you place fillers under ultrasound?

Because it lets the doctor see the vessels and tissue layers in real time. It is the single most important safety measure in filler treatment, and it is standard for every procedure we perform.

Aēstec · Heritage

A practice that began in Amsterdam,
brought to a private floor in Dubai.

You are not walking into a new clinic. You are walking into a ten-year-old Amsterdam practice that happens, now, to be in Dubai.

The origin

In 2015, a group of doctors founded Aēstec on the Zuidas in Amsterdam, out of a shared frustration with how aesthetic medicine was being practised in their city, and a quiet conviction that the discipline deserved a more considered approach.

Over the following ten years, the clinic grew into a second location in Rotterdam and a reputation for declining as many treatments as it performed. The doctors had decided early on that authority is built by refusal as much as by action, and that a face is not a canvas to be filled but a structure to be read.

In 2026, the practice opens its first clinic outside Europe, with the same protocols, the same products, and several of the same doctors who began the work in Amsterdam a decade earlier.

What does not change

Three standards we brought with us
from Amsterdam, unchanged.

Ultrasound guidance

Before any needle approaches the skin, our doctors map the vasculature in real time, so each placement lands in the layer where it belongs, and well away from the vessels it should never touch.

Original, certified products

03

Honest counsel, including no
